## 5. Caveats

- Step 4B kinematics are based on the **inverse-parallax preliminary** distance; they are kept on disk only for sensitivity comparison.
- Step 6B will use **Bailer-Jones r_med_geo as the default distance** for the final kinematic solution. Stars without a BJ match will fall back to 1000/parallax with `distance_source = inverse_parallax_fallback`.
- Inverse-parallax bias grows for `parallax_over_error < 5`; the `q_plx` flag (>5) marks where the two estimates agree well.

## 8. Caveats

- LAMOST RV and Gaia RV are measured at different epochs; binaries and pulsators will show large Δrv. Treat |Δrv| > 50 km/s as a flag for follow-up, not a rejection.
- LAMOST SNR (we use snrg) is a continuum SNR; very low SNR matches with implausible stellar params should be down-weighted in any ML candidate ranking.
- Multi-epoch matches are kept in `hivel_gaia_lamost_matched.csv`. The master table carries only the best epoch per Gaia source, plus `lamost_n_matches`.

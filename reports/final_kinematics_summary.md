## 7. Recommended sample for the paper

- **Primary kinematic results**: `sample_final_strict` + Bailer-Jones distance + Gaia DR3 RV. This is the population for Table 3 and Section 4.2 of the paper.
- **LAMOST RV pass**: a sensitivity check (median ΔV_GSR is small); not the headline number.
- **Background distributions (Toomre, Galactic-coord scatter)**: `sample_gaia_only_clean` + BJ distance.
- **RV outliers (3 stars in master, 2 in followup)**: kept as "possible binaries / variable-RV objects requiring follow-up spectroscopy". Excluded from the headline Table 3.
- **li2023 caveat**: The Li et al. 2023 very-high-velocity subset is retained in the Gaia-only analysis, but none of its 88 objects enters the final Gaia–LAMOST strict sample under our S/N and cross-match criteria. Conclusions involving atmospheric parameters are therefore based primarily on the Li et al. 2021 and Liao et al. 2024 subsets.

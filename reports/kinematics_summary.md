## 6. Caveats

- Stars without Gaia RV (q_rv = False) get RV = 0 in the point estimate; MC sets P_unbound = NaN for them. Treat their V_total as a tangential-only lower bound until LAMOST RVs are merged in Step 5.
- v_esc uses the planar potential at the star's R; off-plane (high |z|) corrections are O(few %).
- 1/parallax is biased for parallax_over_error < ~5; we recommend filtering on q_plx for any kinematics-based inference.

## Take-aways for §5.2

1. None of the Top-3 carries a published variability, binarity or
   spectroscopic-binary tag in SIMBAD as of 2026-04-30. Top-1 is
   present only as a UCAC4 astrometric entry; Top-2 has only the
   Gaia DR3 import; Top-3 has no SIMBAD entry at all.
2. The dual-catalogue inclusion of Top-1 in Li et al. 2021 *and*
   Liao et al. 2024 is the only external corroboration we identify
   for any of the three; the Top-1 P_unbound = 0.996 is also the
   only value above 0.9 in our sample.
3. The absence of variability/binarity flags does NOT constitute
   confirmation of single-star status. Targeted multi-epoch
   spectroscopy remains the natural next step for all three.
